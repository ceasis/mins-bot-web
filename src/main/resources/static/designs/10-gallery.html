— Room II · The Four Works —

Capabilities, on their own plinths.

No. I — Browse

The Wanderer.

Chromium, moving through the web on your behalf; filling forms and reading pages as you might, if you had a second pair of hands and infinite patience.

Medium: session · scope: per-task
No. II — File

The Keeper.

An allow-list is the entire constitution. Nothing happens where you haven't opened a door. Every edit recorded in plain text, reversible on request.

Medium: local fs · scope: allow-list
No. III — Message

The Courier.

Slack, Gmail, Discord — the places where the day's conversations already live. The courier carries your tokens, never ours, and keeps them in the operating system's lockbox.

Medium: direct api · credentials: os keychain
No. IV — Skills

The Library.

Skills are manifests and code, installed as one installs a book. The agent browses its own shelves; you decide what is shelved, and what is taken down.

Medium: manifest+code · source: local/registry

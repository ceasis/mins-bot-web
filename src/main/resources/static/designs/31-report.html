Section I

A letter to the user.

"Running on your own metal, under your own keys, is a principle — and a responsibility."

Dear User,

In the fiscal year ending this spring, Mins Bot performed its duties on your desktop: reading the web, tending your files, answering your correspondence, and running an installable library of skills. We are pleased to report that it performed these duties without routing your data through our infrastructure, because we do not operate any.

Notable accomplishments for the year include: (i) zero server-side retention, (ii) zero telemetry events generated, (iii) zero records touched by any training pipeline, and (iv) one binary, shipped to three platforms. The board considers these achievements to be on-policy.

Looking forward, we intend to continue the practice of collecting nothing. Risks to this policy, as we see them, remain negligible — principally because we have not built the machinery required to change course.

Respectfully,
— The Mins Bot Agent

Section III — Notes to the Report

Notes on data policy.

Note 01 — Data at rest
No copy of user data is held on infrastructure operated by Mins Bot. There is, in fact, no such infrastructure.
Note 02 — Training use
None of your data enters a training pipeline, now or at any future audit.
Note 03 — Credentials
Third-party tokens rest exclusively in the operating system's credential store.
Note 04 — Model calls
Model calls travel direct from user device to the user-selected provider, without interstitial handling.

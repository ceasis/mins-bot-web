Four simple things. Done carefully.

Browse the web.

Only the paths you've allowed. Every move recorded, every edit reversible.

Slack, Gmail, Discord — in your voice, under your rules. Tokens remain where the OS keeps them.

Grow with use.

Skills you install. Habits the agent notices. Nothing sent home.

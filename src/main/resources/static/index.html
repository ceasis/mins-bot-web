§ 03 — Data Policy
p. 03 / 04

Nothing
goes out.

We don't run a server. There is no place for your data to go. Model calls travel direct from your machine to the provider you configure.

Read full policy →
01
Data at rest
On your device only. No copy is held on infrastructure we operate.
02
Credentials
Stored in macOS Keychain, Windows Credential Manager, or libsecret.
03
Training use
None. Your data never enters a training pipeline. Nothing is resold.
04
Model calls
Direct from your machine to the model provider you configure. We don't proxy.
05
Google user data
Processed locally under your consent, scoped to the feature you invoke.
